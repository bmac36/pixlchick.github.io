Pixl Chicks: Design, one pixel at a time

Whether you're looking for a cool new app to help your customers make the most of their shopping experience from whatever device they're using, an updated user-centred website to give your business a fresh new look, or a complete rebrand of your company, Pixl Chicks can handle the job.

Our team of highly creative, technology-savvy developers and designers can bring your wildest ideas to life. We do everything from brand identity to websites to video and print. Yes, we'll even do that wall mural you've been dreaming of adding to your office wall!

Whatever you can dream of, we can bring it to fruition!

Our staff of knowledgable and highly creative people are always happy to help you through any design crisis. All you have do to is ask.

Why Pixl Chicks?

Pixl Chicks is big on ideas, expertise and results but small where it matters — direct and easy to contact and communicate with. We are a team of dedicated, creative-minded and results-driven graphic designers and entrepreneurs. We know that coming up with a great design is only half of what we do. The other half is building relationships, strategies and projects on time and on budget. Whether you are a small business start-up, large commercial enterprise or non-profit, we have the expertise to help you define and achieve your goals.

Giving Back

We're not your average design studio. We're not a group of designers who work endless hours just to increase the bottom line. Sure, we like to be profiltable, but we take a portion of our profits and give back to the community. We get involved. We volunteer — both as a group and as individuals.

To us, if you're not giving back, all you're doing is taking. And that's just bad business.
